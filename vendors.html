Partner With Distributed Energy

We're building the future of solar and battery storage infrastructure. If you're a subcontractor or vendor looking to work with DEI or DER, start by completing our prequalification process.

01

Apply

Complete the prequalification form with your company details, safety record, and capabilities.

02

Review

Our procurement team reviews your submission and documentation within 3–5 business days.

03

Qualify

Approved vendors are added to our qualified vendor list and eligible for project opportunities.

Have These Ready Before You Start
✓Federal Tax ID (EIN)
✓General Liability & Workers Comp info
✓Current COI with expiration date
✓EMR (Experience Modification Rate)
✓W-9 document (PDF)
✓Sample insurance certificate (PDF)
✓OSHA 300 logs — past 3 years
✓Two trade references (GC or owner)
✓Executive & operations contact info
✓Licenses & certifications

If you don't have an executed NDA with Distributed Energy, you'll be able to download our NDA template during the application. Please have it signed and ready to upload.

What Entities Are We?

DEI — Distributed Energy Infrastructure provides EPC services for commercial solar and battery storage projects.

DER — Distributed Energy Resources provides specialty electrical and solar construction services.
